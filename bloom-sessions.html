— 01 / Format

Three things happen.

No lectures, no slide decks. Every Bloom Session follows the same simple shape — so you always know what you're walking into.

01

Show up.

No prep required. Bring a laptop and one real task you'd like to get done — that becomes what we build.

02

Build live.

We share screens and build the same thing at the same time, step by step. You're never just watching — you're doing it too.

03

Leave with a thing.

Every session ends with something that actually runs — an agent, an automation, a page, a workflow. Yours to keep.

— 02 / What we build

Pick a thing to build.

Each track is one focused build, sized to fit a single live session. Come for one, or work through the whole set.

— 03 / Upcoming

Grab a seat.

Sessions are small on purpose. When a date is full, join the waitlist and you'll get first pick of the next one.

— 04 / Who it's for

Made for doers, not spectators.

Bloom is a practice space. If you learn best by making something with your own hands, you'll feel at home.

This is for you if…

  • You've read enough about AI and want to actually use it.
  • You learn by doing, with someone who can answer in the moment.
  • You want a small, friendly room — not a webinar with 500 people.
  • You'd rather leave with one working thing than ten bookmarked tabs.

Maybe not, if…

  • You want a passive lecture to watch later on 2× speed.
  • You're after deep code — Bloom is practical, not engineering-heavy.
  • You can't make a live slot. Replays come later, but the room is the point.
