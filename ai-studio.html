— 02 / How we work

From idea to a
working version.

A small, transparent process. You always know what's being built, why, and what comes next.

01
Listen
Understand the people and the real problem — not just the brief. We watch how the work actually happens today.
02
Frame
One page that names the problem and the choices. Nothing more. We agree on what "done" looks like before we build.
03
Prototype
A working version in front of real users, fast. We keep what works and cut what doesn't, in the open.
04
Ship
A small, reliable version that earns the right to grow — handed over with the docs your team needs to own it.

And we train your team to take the wheel.
