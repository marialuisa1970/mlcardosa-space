— 01 / Method

Three words. That's the method.

Most AI content is loud and shallow. The Lab is the opposite. Three principles, applied to everything we publish.

01

Slow.

No hot takes, no hacks. We sit with a question until it gets simpler.

02

Specific.

Real prompts. Real outputs. Real mistakes. We show the work, not the highlight reel.

03

Practical.

Every piece ends with something you can try Monday — or decide to skip with reasons.

— 02 / Courses

Pick your level.

Six courses, three levels. Self-paced, with live cohort weeks twice a year. No certificates — just better thinking.

— 03 / Playbooks

Field notes for working with AI.

Short essays with prompts attached. Each playbook is a way of thinking — written down, tested, sharable.

— 04 / Sessions

Live & recorded.

Office hours, workshops, conversations with people doing the work. Live attendance is small — replays are open.
